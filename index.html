Talk Overview

  • Research I performed which became a start up company, PneumaCare Ltd, of which I am a founding shareholder.
  • Company core IP based on a technique which I invented called Structured Light Plethysmography. (WIPO patent: WO2010066824.)
  • The PneumaScan is a non-invasive monitoring device which was, like much of modern technology, inspired by science fiction.
  • Research I started a few years ago; interesting here because it has moved further along the road to wide-scale adoption. My current research is similarly based on computer vision.
